Common questions

Is it really free?▾
Yes. ohYaaa is free to use with no credit card required and no trial period. No paywalls blocking core features.
Is my data secure?▾
Your data is stored on Google Firebase with industry-standard encryption. Only you can access your trading data — we can't see it, and we'll never sell it.
What markets does it support?▾
Stocks, options, futures, forex, and crypto. You can log any asset type and track performance across all of them.
Can I import trades from my broker?▾
Not yet — broker import is on the roadmap. For now, the three input methods (entry/exit, direct P&L, daily total) make manual logging fast. Most trades take under 10 seconds to log.
How is this different from a spreadsheet?▾
Spreadsheets require you to build and maintain formulas, charts, and dashboards yourself. ohYaaa calculates everything automatically — win rate, profit factor, P&L by setup, mistake costs, equity curves — the moment you log a trade. Zero formulas. Zero maintenance.
