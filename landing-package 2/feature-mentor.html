Why Mentorship Matters

Trading is hard enough without learning everything the hard way. A mentor who's already made (and learned from) the mistakes can help you skip years of painful trial and error. They see patterns in your trading that you can't see yourself.

ohYaaa's built-in mentorship system connects students with experienced traders. Share your journal, get feedback on specific trades, receive homework assignments, and stay accountable with regular check-ins — all within the platform.

Flag Trades for Review

Took a trade you're unsure about? Flag it for your mentor to review. They'll see your entry, exit, notes, and screenshot — everything they need to give you targeted feedback.

  • One-click flag for mentor review
  • Add specific questions about the trade
  • Get detailed written feedback
Homework

Receive Assignments

Your mentor can assign specific tasks to help you work on weak spots. "Only trade breakouts this week" or "Paper trade 10 reversals" — structured practice that builds skills.

  • Custom assignments from your mentor
  • Track completion progress
  • Get graded with mentor feedback
Accountability

Weekly Check-ins

Schedule regular sessions with your mentor. Review your week, discuss challenges, set goals for next week. Consistent accountability is the secret to faster improvement.
