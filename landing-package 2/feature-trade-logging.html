Three ways to log

Choose the method that matches your data. Switch anytime.

Method 1

Entry/Exit Prices

The traditional method. Enter your entry price, exit price, and position size — we calculate your P&L automatically. Perfect if you have detailed execution data from your broker.

  • Auto-calculated P&L
  • Supports long and short positions
  • Trade length calculated from dates
Method 2

Direct P&L Input

Just know you made $150 on that trade? Enter it directly. No need to dig up entry and exit prices. Perfect for traders who get P&L from their broker's trade history.

  • Single field entry
  • Positive or negative values
  • Fastest logging method
Method 3

Daily Total

Enter your running daily P&L total, and we'll calculate what this trade contributed. Ideal for scalpers or traders who only check their account balance at end of day.

  • Enter daily total, we do the math
  • Shows other trades on that day
  • Real-time calculation preview
